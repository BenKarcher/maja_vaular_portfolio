This assignment was my first introduction to HTML and CSS. Our job was to create a campaign website for the packaging we had redesigned previously.

FOCUS ON FUN

The website has to reflect the product's playfulness, and this was used in the design by having everything look like it is stacked on top of each other and having these fun visuals such as the footer being the iced coffee, or the floating product pictures stuck on the side of the pages.
