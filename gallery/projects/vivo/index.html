MY WORK IN VIVO

My time with vivo allowed me to work with other designers and follow vivo’s visual guidelines. Which was a great opportunity. This however does mean that some of the work here wasn’t fully done by me, but rather a collaboration between me, agencies or other designers.
On the right you see two Key Visuals for vivo’s product Y17s. My job on this was refining the typography and placement, and choosing the background colors. While the picture / visual itself was made by Elina Condrasov and Roberto Tomasi and Jesus Navas.

Young and fresh poster

This poster was made by me based on vivo’s guidelines. The photograph and visual was made by Elina Condrasov and Roberto Tomasi and Jesus Navas.

A TRIBUTE TO WOMEN

I got tasked to design a women’s day invitation card. I decided to use pink to show that pink is a good strong color which is often looked down on because of its popularity with women. I illustrated 3 diverse women. An athlete, a businesswoman and a student. This is a reference to big accomplishments through the feminist movement.
