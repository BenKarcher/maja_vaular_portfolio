BOOK REDESIGN

In this school assignment I got to redesign the book «Alle utlendinger har lukka gardiner» by Maria Navarro Skaranger. This included the book cover and the first few pages of the book.
The book is a book for teenagers and it takes place in a pretty urban area. The title of the book translates to «All foreigners have closed curtains» and it gives us a peak into the main characters life.

EASY TO READ

The book is designed to be easy to read as the way The book uses language that’s easy to understand for people who don’t read a lot, so I wanted to continue this in the inside design of the book. I did this by using readable typefaces and slightly bigger text sizes than what is normal, as well as lots of clear space.
